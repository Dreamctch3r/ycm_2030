Youth Consciousness Movement is inspiring active citizens and providing a platform for them to develop their community and their country.

Empower

YCM is a movement which aims to create active citizens who control their own destiny through a large scale awareness campaign.

Fund

YCM is an impact investment firm that brings together individuals solving problems in their communities and funds their ventures.

Equip

YCM is a research and technology company dedicated to discovering and developing solutions to pervasive problems.
